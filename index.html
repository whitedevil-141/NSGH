About Us

We Are Specialized in Medical Diagnostics

New Shafipur General Hospital is dedicated to providing world-class healthcare services to the people of Bangladesh. With a team of experienced doctors, skilled medical staff, and state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities, we ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans.
